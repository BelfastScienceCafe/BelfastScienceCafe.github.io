Prof. Aaron Maule - QUB - 'A Tale of Pabulum, Plague, Poverty & Plagiarism'

Tue 19 November 2013
By Eoin Travers

In Events.

tags: events

On Thursday November 19th, QUB's Professor Aaron Maule will present "Parasitic Worms: A Tale of Pabulum, Plague, Poverty & Plagiarism", in association with Giving What We Can: QUB, and the Belfast Science Cafe.

The event will take place in the Parlour Bar, Elmwood Avenue, from 7PM.

Entry is Free, as always.

Hope to see you all there!
