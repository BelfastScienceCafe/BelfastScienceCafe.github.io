About Us

Belfast Science Cafe bring the latest ideas in science and technology into a relaxed and informal setting. For the (optional) price of a beer, wine, or coffee, you can hear our invited speakers' insights on a topic close to their hearts, followed by open discussion, and an informal chat over a few drinks.
