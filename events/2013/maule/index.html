Prof. Aaron Maule - QUB - 'A Tale of Pabulum, Plague, Poverty & Plagiarism'

Tue 19 November 2013
By Eoin Travers

In Events.

tags: events

On Thursday November 21st, QUB's Professor Aaron Maule will present "Parasitic Worms: A Tale of Pabulum, Plague, Poverty & Plagiarism", in association with Giving What We Can: QUB, and the Belfast Science Cafe.

Come to this to discover some of the horrors of human parasitic diseases, as well as what we can do about them!

The event will take place in the Parlour Bar, Elmwood Avenue, from 7PM. (Map)

Entry is Free, as always.

Hope to see you all there!
